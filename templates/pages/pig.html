What is Pig Latin?

It would be nice if Pig Latin was a genuine language and in some ways it is. But in reality it is a language game where English words are altered by moving the first letter to the end of the word and adding on a syllable to the end of the word.

There are a variety of methods to accomplish this translation, with some methods dating back to the 1500s. We use the classic 'ay' technique, but there are many others.
